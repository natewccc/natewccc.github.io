Constructing An Eco-Friendly Home

"What even is an eco-friendly home?"

An eco-friendly home is a home that uses materials and technology in a way that is
designed to reduce carbon footprint and be overall better for the environment.

Eco-friendly homes have features such as...

  • Sustainable/locally sources materials
  • Solar panels and other renewable energy
  • Geothermal heating & cooling
  • LED lighting
